Life in the future (未来的生活)

2023-12-23 英语写作

With the development of science and technology, life in the future will be quite different.

First of all, the environment that we live in will become better and better. There will be no heavy traffic in our cities so that we can drive smoothly.

In the second place, we may be able to go to other planets by spaceship or build many buildings there.

At the same time, we needn't to do dangerous work for ourselves as artificial intelligence develops rapidly. Because robots will replace us to do it.

The most important, we will put to use new energy that is inexhaustible in the future.

译文：
随着科学技术的发展，未来的生活将会截然不同。首先，我们生活的环境会变得越来越好。我们的城市将没有拥挤的交通以便于我们可以顺畅地行驶。其次，我们可能能够乘宇宙飞船去其他行星，或是在那里建造许多建筑。与此同时，随着人工智能的快速发展，我们不需要亲自做危险的工作。因为机器人会代替我们做这些工作。最重要的是，我们将在未来投入使用取之不尽的新能源。

do sth. for oneself 为某人做某事
artificial intelligence n. 人工智能
put to use 运用，投入使用
inexhaustible adj. 取之不尽的
